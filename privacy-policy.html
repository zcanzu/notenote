Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 4, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to NoteNote ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

2.1 User Account Information

  • User ID: A unique identifier (UUID) assigned to your account
  • Display Name: An optional name you choose to display (if provided)
  • Matching Code: A temporary 6-character code used for pairing with other users (expires after 30 seconds)
  • Account Creation Date: Timestamp of when your account was created

2.2 Device Information

  • Device Token: A unique identifier for push notifications (stored only when you enable notifications)
  • Device ID: Used for app functionality and service delivery

2.3 Content You Create

  • Notes: Text messages and drawings you create and share with your matched partner
  • Images: Drawings or images you upload (stored securely in Firebase Storage)
  • Channel Information: Data related to your matched connections, including channel IDs and partner information

2.4 Usage Data

  • Information about how you interact with the App
  • Timestamps of your activities (note creation, updates, etc.)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To enable real-time note sharing between matched users
  • User Matching: To facilitate pairing between users using temporary matching codes
  • Push Notifications: To send you notifications when your partner creates or updates a note (only if you enable notifications)
  • App Functionality: To maintain your account, manage channels, and provide core features
  • Widget Updates: To update your home screen widget with the latest notes
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services

4. How We Store Your Information

Your information is stored securely using the following services:

  • Firebase Firestore: User accounts, channels, and notes are stored in Google's Firebase Firestore database
  • Firebase Storage: Images and drawings are stored in Firebase Storage
  • Local Device Storage: Some information (user ID, channel ID) is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults for app functionality

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard security measures.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

5.1 With Your Matched Partner

When you match with another user, the following information is shared:

  • Your display name (if provided)
  • Notes and drawings you create
  • Channel information necessary for real-time synchronization

Important: Your User ID and other personal identifiers are not directly shared with your partner, but are used internally to manage your connection.

5.2 With Service Providers

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For data storage, real-time synchronization, and push notifications
  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs): For delivering push notifications to iOS devices

These service providers are bound by their own privacy policies and security standards.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

6. Data Retention

  • Active Channels: Data is retained while your channel is active (up to 12 hours by default, or as you configure)
  • Expired Channels: Channel data may be automatically deleted after expiration
  • User Accounts: Your account information is retained until you request deletion
  • Notes: The last 10 notes per channel are retained for history purposes

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can view your account information and notes within the App
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Push Notifications: You can enable or disable push notifications in your device settings
  • Display Name: You can update or remove your display name at any time
  • Channel Management: You can end a match at any time, which will stop sharing with that partner

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. By using our App, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country.

10. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

11. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest for stored data
  • Secure authentication and authorization
  • Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
