Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I match with another user?

A: To match with another user:

  1. Open the NoteNote app
  2. You'll see a 6-character matching code (e.g., "ABC123")
  3. Share this code with your partner
  4. Your partner enters your code in the "Enter Partner Code" field
  5. Tap "Match" to create a connection
  6. Once matched, you can start sharing notes!

Note: Matching codes expire after 30 seconds. If your code expires, tap the refresh button to generate a new one.

Q: Can I scan a QR code to match?

A: Yes! NoteNote supports QR code scanning:

  1. Tap the QR code icon in the matching screen
  2. Allow camera access when prompted
  3. Point your camera at your partner's QR code
  4. The app will automatically match you
Q: How long does a match last?

A: By default, matches last 24 hours. However, you can customize the duration:

  • Go to the matching screen
  • Select your preferred duration (1-12 hours)
  • The match will expire after the selected time

You can also manually end a match at any time by tapping "End Match".

Q: Can I create drawings or just text notes?

A: NoteNote supports both text notes and drawings:

  • Text Notes: Tap "Text" and type your message
  • Drawings: Tap "Drawing" to open the drawing canvas. You can draw with your finger or Apple Pencil

Both types of notes are shared in real-time with your matched partner.

Q: Why am I not receiving push notifications?

A: To receive push notifications:

  1. Make sure you've granted notification permissions when the app first asked
  2. Check your device Settings → NoteNote → Notifications
  3. Ensure "Allow Notifications" is enabled
  4. Make sure your device is connected to the internet

If you're still not receiving notifications, try restarting the app or your device.

Q: How does the widget work?

A: The NoteNote widget shows your latest note on your home screen:

  1. Long press on your home screen
  2. Tap the "+" button in the top-left corner
  3. Search for "NoteNote"
  4. Select the widget size you prefer
  5. Tap "Add Widget"

The widget automatically updates when you or your partner creates a new note.

Q: Can I delete my notes?

A: Yes, you can delete your own notes:

  1. Open the note you want to delete
  2. Tap the delete button (trash icon)
  3. Confirm the deletion

Note: You can only delete notes that you created. You cannot delete notes created by your partner.

Q: What happens to my data when a match expires?

A: When a match expires:

  • The channel becomes inactive
  • You can no longer send new notes to that partner
  • Your note history may be automatically deleted after a period of time
  • You can start a new match with the same or a different partner
Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes, we take your privacy and security seriously:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Data is stored securely using Firebase (Google's secure cloud infrastructure)
  • Only you and your matched partner can see your shared notes
  • We do not share your data with third parties except as described in our Privacy Policy
Q: Can I use NoteNote on multiple devices?

A: Each device creates a separate account. If you want to use NoteNote on multiple devices, you'll need to match separately on each device. Your matches and notes are not synced across devices.
